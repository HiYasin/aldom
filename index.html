Your donation can make a difference. Join us in saving humanitys.

Why Donate

Blood donation is a simple, yet powerful act that can save lives.Every two seconds, someone in the world needs blood.By donating, you can help patients undergoing surgery, recovering from accidents, battling cancer, and suffering from chronic illnesses.Your contribution makes a critical difference and provides hope to those in need.

Benefits of Donating Blood

Save Lives

One donation can save up to three lives.

Health Benefits

Regular donation can help reduce the risk of certain health conditions, like heart disease.

Community Support

Your donation helps ensure a stable blood supply in your community

Free Health Screening

Every donation includes a mini health check-up, including blood pressure, hemoglobin levels, and more.

Eligibility Criteria

Age

You must be between 17 and 65 years old.

Weight

You must weigh at least 50 kg

Health

You must be in good general health. Certain medical conditions and medications may affect your eligibility

Recent Activity

You should not have donated blood in the last 120 days
